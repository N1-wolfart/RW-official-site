How to Join RW

RW Member Admission Test Rules. Follow the steps below to become a member.

Step 1: Fruit

Take a photo of the fruit at the gatehouse and ping <@🍎fruits> to report it with the photo.

Step 2: TA

Capture the flag in the Arena within 13 seconds.
Submit a video to the examiner, or have the examiner accompany you during the test.

Step 3: Skill Assessment

Win a 1v1 “first-to-10” match against a test <@examiner>.
Maps: Castle or Arena / Bow: any bow.
Regeneration is limited to 12.
Examiners are officially recognized clan members.
If you fail, you may retake no sooner than one week later. After failing twice, wait 1–2 months before trying again.

Step 4: Trustworthiness Assessment

Your behavior, attitude, and manners within the clan will be evaluated based on:
- Any history of rule violations
- Communication skills
- Sense of responsibility and willingness to report issues
You will pass if at least 60% of current members approve you in a trust vote.

Rules After Admission

If you break rules or undermine trust, the leader and sub-leaders will deliberate and you may be banned depending on the outcome.

Notes

Test and operational rules may be revised at any time depending on the state of the clan.
